Seat Leon 1P FR brake conversionDRAFT

Making Seat’s stop

I’m upgrading my Seat Leon 1P non FR pre facelift brakes to FR brakes & Brembo drilled discs.

What you need

If you already have 288mm front disks, the front calipers will work, you will then only need the caliper mounts.

Rear calipers are different size, so they do not work.

I painted mine with metallic grey, so I got least rusty FR brakes with brackets I could find from a crashed Leon.

Part numbers

Part numbers for all the extra parts (not including the calipers/brackets/pistons etc) you will need to fit larger brakes

  • 312mm front brakes:

    • Discs: BREMBO 09.9772.1X
    • Piston seal & dust boot: VAG 1K0698471B
    • Brake carrier to hub: VAG N90948802
    • Disc dust shields: right 1K0615312F, left 1K0615311F

  • 282mm rear brakes:

    • Discs: BREMBO 08.A202.1X
    • Piston seal & dust boot: VAG 8D0698671
    • Brake carrier to hub: VAG N91006802
    • Disc dust shields: right 1K0615612P, left 1K0615611P
